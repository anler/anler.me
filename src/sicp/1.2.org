Thanslate the following expression into prefix form:

\begin{equation}
\frac{5 + 4 + (2 - (3 - (6 + \frac{4}{5})))}{3(6 - 2)(2 - 7)}
\end{equation}

* Solution

#+BEGIN_SRC scheme
  (/ (+ 5
        4
        (- 2 (- 3 (+ 6 (/ 4 5)))))
     (* 3
        (- 6 2)
        (- 7 2)))
#+END_SRC

Result is -0.24666666666666667.
